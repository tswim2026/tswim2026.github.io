TSWIM 2026 & NSTC Management II Division Research Workshop

Reasons to Submit and/or Attend TSWIM2025:

Review Stage:

  1. To obtain constructive review suggestions from scholars who have published in very good journals

On-site Stage (for presenter):

  1. To obtain on-site feedback with editors and top scholars before submitting to top journals
  2. To have ample time to interact with editors and top scholars
  3. To obtain inspiration for new research

On-site Stage (for participant):

  1. To learn from quality presentations and comments by editors and top scholars
  2. To make friends with international and Taiwanese scholars, enabling possible collaboration

After TSWIM:

  1. Free to proceed to journal submission without duplicate publication issues
  2. Upon authors' agreement, high quality paper will be recommended to PAJAIS (Pacific Asia Journal of the Association for Information Systems) for fast-track review

Submission to this TSWIM indicates that the authors understand:

  1. This TSWIM does not require copyright from authors. By submitting to TSWIM, the authors granted TSWIM to have their names and paper titles appear on the conference webpage.
  2. This TSWIM will not publish abstract nor the main text. This arrangement facilitates future submissions to journals, after TSWIM.
  3. This TSWIM particularly welcomes submissions that are aimed to be published in "Senior Scholars' List of Premier Journals" of the Association for Information Systems (AIS) (https://aisnet.org/page/SeniorScholarListofPremierJournals).
